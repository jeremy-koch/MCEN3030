## How the instructional team will use GitHub

We will additionally be tracking the commit history for every student in the course. While it is not a perfect indicator, commit history is an interesting proxy for student effort and learning:
- One commit early in the week suggests the student looked at the assignment but hardly got started.
- Five commits over the course of the week suggests the student is engaged with the assignment and is likely learning from it.
- One commit Friday at 11:51PM suggests genius. Just kidding, these students probably misused AI, or at least has bad commit habits.
